Ephemeral

by: Winter Ramos

Ephemeral like drops of dew on a peddle of an untouched flower Ephemeral like the brush of skin from a touch of a woman's hand (As the sun hits the liquid rolls down the pedal and drops to the dirt below) (And the simple touch stops breath and with a wink is gone) Ephemeral like a laugh in a nighttime bar as it closes To tears in the empty street of dawn and fog

Ephemeral like a woman's lips on your spine for the last time

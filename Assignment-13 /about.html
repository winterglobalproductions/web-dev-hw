“Style”

Winter Ramos

Hailing from the kingdom of North Idaho,

Winter Ramos comes from modest roots in a small cabin.

From there he moved onto become an enthusiastic adventurist who is comfortable in any environment,

has traveled to nearly 40 countries, climbed in most of the worlds mountain ranges and is equally confortable in large cities sipping elegant cocktails. Winter has more than 10 years experience working rope access in industrial settings, rigging for safety, and experiences in more artistic aspects of working at heights with dance companies and concerts.

Winter is currently at the University of Montana School of Journalism. He has creative writing, photography, multi-media and marketing skills. Winter works and excels in multi-platform, multi-language, multi-culture environments globally.

His extensive travels and exotic experiences have led him to an intriguing life that adds depth and character to work places, parties and unusual assignments.

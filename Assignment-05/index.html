Bacon + Whiskey + Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Ice Cream
  • Whiskey

Directions

  1. Place Two Scoops of Ice Cream in bowl
  2. Oven roast bacon bits until lightly crunchy
  3. Sprinkle bacon bits over ice cream
  4. Open bottle of good bourbon whiskey
  5. Pour over bowl of ice cream and bacon
  6. Eat

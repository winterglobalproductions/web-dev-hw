Glamorous Cardomon and Hibiscus Infused Coctail


Prep Time Difficulty Taste level
One Month Extremely Challenging Seven Stars

Ingredients

  • Rum
  • Hibiscus Flowers
  • Cardomon Pods
  • Fresh Lime Lime Leaves
  • Lime Juice
  • Simple Syrup

Directions

  1. Add Ingredients and mix
  2. Let sit for one month or more
  3. Shake With ice and bitters
  4. Add simple syrup and lime juice
  5. Garnish with a Rose Pedal
  6. Drink
